About

I’m a Full Stack Javascript developer from Kirkland, WA. I’ve been programming in some capacity for ~7 years with experience in Java, PHP, C#, and Python. JavaScript is my preferred language because of it’s fast-paced, vibrant community and out-of-the-box support for async and first-class functions. I’m proficient in standards like JQuery as well as modern technologies such as React, Redux, ECMAScript 2015 and build/code quality tools like Gulp and ESLint.

In my spare time you’ll find me taking pictures of my cat, drinking tea, and watching Formula 1.
